title: Origin and involvement levels
---

DAIU 1.0 combines two dimensions:

- **Origin** (Human Made or AI Generated)
- **Involvement level** (None, Assist, Remix, Major, Full)

## Origin

- **HM – Human Made**  
  First substantial draft or base content was created by one or more people.

- **AG – AI Generated**  
  First substantial draft or base content was created primarily by an AI system.

## Involvement levels

### HM · None <span id="hm-none"></span>

Human origin with no AI involvement.

```text
Origin: HM · None — DAIU 1.0
```

---

### HM · Assist <span id="hm-assist"></span>

Human origin. AI tools used for supportive work such as grammar suggestions, clean up, resizing or minor edits.

```text
Origin: HM · Assist — DAIU 1.0
```

---

### HM · Remix <span id="hm-remix"></span>

Human origin. AI used to transform or remix existing human created material while the person directs the result.

```text
Origin: HM · Remix — DAIU 1.0
```

---

### AG · Remix <span id="ag-remix"></span>

AI origin. People used AI outputs as source and directed additional transformation, blending or adaptation.

```text
Origin: AG · Remix — DAIU 1.0
```

---

### AG · Major <span id="ag-major"></span>

AI origin. AI systems produced most of the content, with human input focused on prompts, curation or light edits.

```text
Origin: AG · Major — DAIU 1.0
```

---

### AG · Full <span id="ag-full"></span>

Content is shown almost exactly as generated by AI with no or minimal human edits.

Examples include:

- a raw image from a generative model published as is  
- a ChatGPT story posted verbatim  
- an AI generated song released unedited  
- a short AI generated video clip uploaded without changes  

```text
Origin: AG · Full — DAIU 1.0
```
