title: Glossary
---

**AI system**  
A model or service that generates or transforms content in response to input.

**Origin**  
Whether the base draft or primary content was first created by a person or by an AI system.

**Involvement level**  
A description of how much AI shaped the final result.

**Disclosure**  
A short statement that explains how AI was used.

**DAIU**  
Declare AI Usage, an open standard for describing how AI contributed to a specific piece of content.
