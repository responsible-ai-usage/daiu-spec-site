This section summarizes DAIU brand guidance for implementations, documentation and tooling.

## Name and tagline

- Short name: **DAIU (Declare AI Usage)**
- Full: **Responsible AI Usage Foundation, Inc.**
- Tagline: **Be Transparent About AI Use**

## Colors

- Core green: `#3D7A4E`  
- Page background: `#FBFAF3`  
- Text: `#141414`

Human origin badges:

- Background: light green tint (for example `#E2F3E7`)
- Border: slightly darker (`#C2DFC7`)

AI origin badges:

- Background: light teal or blue tint (for example `#E3F4F8`)
- Border: slightly darker (`#C1DEE9`)

## Typography

- Headings: **Vollkorn Serif**
- Body: **Noto Serif**
- Code: a clean monospaced font

## Tone

The DAIU specification and examples should remain:

- neutral and descriptive
- non judgmental
- clear and concise

The standard does not label content as good, bad, authentic or fake. It describes how people and AI contributed.
